Mission causes

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-profit organization based in Pakistan. Its mission is to alleviate poverty and provide assistance to those in need through various social welfare programs. Saylani Mission focuses on providing food, healthcare, education, and vocational training to underprivileged individuals and communities.

Food Distribution:

Saylani operates soup kitchens and food distribution centers to provide meals to the homeless and impoverished individuals across Pakistan.

Healthcare Services:

They offer healthcare services through mobile medical camps, dispensaries, and hospitals, providing medical assistance to those who cannot afford it.

Education Programs:

Saylani runs schools and educational programs to provide free or subsidized education to children from low-income families. They also offer vocational training courses to equip individuals with skills for employment opportunities.

Disaster Relief:

Saylani participates in disaster relief efforts, providing aid and assistance to communities affected by natural disasters such as floods, earthquakes, and droughts.

Marriage Support Program:

Saylani provides support to financially disadvantaged couples by organizing mass marriages where wedding expenses are covered by the organization.
